Fluffy Gluten-free Pancakes


Gluten free pancakes don't have to be plain or boring, theses fluffy
pancakes are spiced with cinnamon, and vanilla for extra flavor.
These pancakes are easy to make for a delcious breakfast or brunch
with their soft and tender texture paired with your favorite syrup
and fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose gluten-free flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons gluten-free baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons of melted, unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup cold milk, plus more if needed
  • cooking spray or additional butter for greasing

Directions

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients
  2. Step 2: Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and sugar together in a medium bowl
  3. Step 3: In a seperate bowl, whisk egg, melted butter, vanilla and milk together until smooth
  4. Step 4: Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients; stir just until combined (batter should be slightly lumpy).
    Let batter rest for 5 to 10 minutes to thicken. If the batter seems too thick to pour, add 1 to 2 tablespoons more milk
  5. Step 5: Spray the griddle with cooking spray or add a small amount of butter
    and preheat over medium-low heat
  6. Step 6: Pour about 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto the preheataed griddle. Cook until
    bubbles form on top and edges look set, about 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook until golden
    on the second side, 1 to 2 more minutes. Serve warm.
